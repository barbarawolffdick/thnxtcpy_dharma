The Next Company

What

An initiative that will explore business opportunities in the mobile arena by creating an environment that will facilitate the selection and prototyping, the MVP building, the budgeting process, the funding strategy and the implementation of innovative mobile businesses.

TheNextCompany is initiative of Grupo ABC with the support of Tesla

How it works

We will receive and select projects of mobile businesses

Validate the entrepreneurs and the business plans

Select the winners and lead investment rounds

Support the startup phase of the companies
